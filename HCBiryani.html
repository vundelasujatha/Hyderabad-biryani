Hyderabad Chicken Biryani in 4 Steps

Step 1 : Biryani Masala

In a Pan take 3 bay leafs, 2-3 cloves, 2-3 green cardamoms, cinnamon sticks, and corrainder powder raost them well.now grind these ingredients

Step 2 : Marinate

Take chicken in a bowl, add salt, ginger-garlic paste, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, biryani masala , yogurt and fried onions and mix well. marinate chicken for 2 hours in defridge. now in a bowl add 3 spoons oil and heat , add marinated chicken cook for 15 minutes until oil seperates chicken.

Step 3 : Cook Rice

Take 4 glasses of Basmati rice in a bowl and wash thoroughly 2-3 times.Heat sufficient water in a deep bowl. now add rice and cook for 30 minutes

Step 4 : Mix up

take a bowl add cooked chicken in one layer and add rice in 2nd layer , then again chicken and then rice as 4 layers sprinkle half a glass water and saffron mix water ,cook for 15 minutes in low flame. now biryani is ready to serve.
